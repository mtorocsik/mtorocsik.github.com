Bio

Two Fisted Law is punk rock for by and of Punk Rock Fans!

Two Fisted Law, dating back to 2001, has gone through many changes. With guitarist, Rick, and bassist, Dave, being the only original members from the beginning, there have been many improving changes to this band. Kyle, Dave, and Rick have been playing in music projects together for years. Their familiarity with each other helps them work together to produce something that the band is always proud of. Matt and Jym Joined the band in 2007. these changes to the original lineup have given the band a more unique feel, building on the road paved all of the classic punk bands who have influenced the band and completing the immense sound that is Two Fisted Law.
